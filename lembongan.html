Villa Lembongan

The Villa

A clifftop sanctuary above the Indian Ocean

Perched on the dramatic sunset cliffs of Nusa Lembongan, Villa Lembongan offers an experience unlike any other in Bali. Five spacious bedrooms open to sweeping ocean panoramas, each designed to blur the boundary between inside and out.

The centrepiece is a breathtaking infinity pool that appears to merge seamlessly with the horizon — a place to drift, to reflect, and to watch the sun dissolve into the sea each evening.

Your private chef will craft personalised menus drawing on the finest local produce, while the dedicated housekeeping team ensures every moment of your stay is effortless.

Villa Details

Everything You Need to Know

Welcome drink and fresh towels upon check-in. Pick up and drop off from Lembongan's boat pier. Daily breakfast prepared from our menu (8:00 AM to 10:00 AM). Daily housekeeping (7:00 AM to 8:00 PM). Dedicated villa manager reachable anytime. Night security guard (9:00 PM to 6:00 AM).
Five bedrooms with king-size beds and ensuite bathrooms with ocean views. All bedrooms are soundproofed. Master suite includes bathtub with ocean view. Bedroom 3 can be configured as family suite with kids' bunk beds.
Architect-designed by a renowned French architect. Interiors curated by a French interior designer. Custom-made furnishings with soft linens, natural fibers, and artisan-crafted pieces. Seamless indoor-outdoor layout with natural materials including stone, wood, and textured finishes.
Open-plan living and dining. Uninterrupted 270 degree ocean and sunset views. 22-metre infinity pool (largest on the island). Two private plunge pools. Petanque court, billiard table, kids' board game corner. Wine fridge, Nespresso machine, SONY sound system, fast Starlink Wi-Fi. Safety box and hair dryer in every bedroom. BBQ equipment. Electrical generator. Exterior CCTV.
Light-filled open-concept living and dining with panoramic ocean views. Designer kitchen for private chef experiences. Bar and entertainment area with billiard table, wine fridge, cigar selection. Dining for 10 guests overlooking the ocean. Multiple sun decks with private sunbeds outside each room. Fire-pit terrace with sunset views. Upper-level deck with bean bags for stargazing.
Private chef. BBQ. In-house massages with ocean view. Private yoga, boxing, and fitness sessions. Pool fence rental. Jet ski adventures (10% discount for guests). Additional pastries from Let's Bake (one day advance order).
Fast boats depart daily from Sanur Harbour to Nusa Lembongan. Preferred arrival at Jungutbatu Beach or Mushroom Bay, with 20+ round trips per day. Our team will welcome you at the harbor and escort you to the villa.
Check-in from 2:00 PM. Check-out until 11:00 AM. Luggage storage available before check-in or after check-out at the villa or at Let's Bake Cafe on Jungutbatu beach.
The Shambala is a completely private villa with exclusive use, private entrance, and private parking. Fireworks are strictly forbidden by law on the island. Pets are not allowed.
