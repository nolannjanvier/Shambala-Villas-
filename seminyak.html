Villa Seminyak

The Villa

Traditional architecture, contemporary luxury

Set in the heart of Seminyak — Bali's most coveted address — Villa Seminyak is a masterpiece of traditional Javanese joglo architecture reimagined for modern luxury living. Towering hand-carved timber pavilions rise above lush tropical gardens, creating an atmosphere of extraordinary calm.

A spectacular 20-metre swimming pool forms the heart of the estate, flanked by sun loungers, an outdoor dining pavilion, and a BBQ terrace shaded by towering palms. Five generous ensuite bedrooms, each with its own character and garden outlook, ensure total privacy for every guest.

Just minutes from Oberoi Street, Ku De Ta, and Seminyak's finest dining, yet completely removed from the world — a rare balance that only Shambala can offer.
